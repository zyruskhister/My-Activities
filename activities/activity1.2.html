Shakespeare’s Romeo and Juliet

The Five Acts

Play Structure

1. Exposition

Introduction of Verona, feud between Montagues and Capulets, Romeo loves Rosaline.

2. Rising Action

Romeo meets Juliet
Balcony Scene
Secret Marriage

3. Climax

1. Tybalt kills Mercutio
2. Romeo kills Tybalt
3. Romeo is banished

4. Falling Action

1. Juliet’s arranged marriage to Paris
2. Friar’s potion plan

5. Resolution

Romeo and Juliet die
Families reconcile
